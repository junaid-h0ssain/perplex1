Real Stories from Real Farmers

Hear directly from the farmers who are using HarvestGuard to protect their harvests and improve their livelihoods.

Rafique, Barishal

Last year, sudden rains destroyed 40% of my rice. This year, HarvestGuard warned me in advance—I covered my grains. 90% of my harvest was saved.

Shamima, Rajshahi

When the temperature rises, rice changes color. Following the app's advice, I stored my crops higher. Losses have reduced significantly, and grain spillage has decreased.

Jalal, Mymensingh

I used to not know which day was best to dry rice. Now I check the app and decide. Problems are reducing. My confidence in crop care has also increased.

Ayesha, Khulna

The AI scanner helped me identify rotten crops before they spread. I separated them immediately and saved the rest of my harvest. This technology is a game-changer!

Karim, Sylhet

Weather forecasts in Bangla made it so easy to understand. I can now plan my storage better and avoid last-minute panic. My income has improved by 25% this season.

Fatima, Rangpur

The offline feature is amazing! Even when I don't have internet, I can still access important advisories. HarvestGuard truly understands our needs.
