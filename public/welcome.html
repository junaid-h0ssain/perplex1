Reducing Food Loss in Bangladesh

Protecting Every Grain, Securing Every Future.

Bangladesh loses 4.5 million metric tonnes of food grains annually—costing $1.5 billion. HarvestGuard helps farmers reduce post-harvest losses through smart storage monitoring and real-time weather advisories.

The Challenge

Food Loss Undermines Food Security

Inadequate storage facilities, poor handling practices, and inefficient transportation result in massive economic and environmental costs. These losses particularly affect vulnerable communities and hinder progress toward SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production.
