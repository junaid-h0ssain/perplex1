Research Behind KrishiRokkha

Our team conducted extensive research on food loss across Bangladesh. Data from BIDS, FAO, and Financial Express shows that 12–32% of staple food is lost every year during post-harvest stages.

Key Findings

  • Weather unpredictability causes grain damage.
  • Poor storage bags increase moisture & mold.
  • Farmers lack real-time guidance.
  • Existing solutions are too complex.

Technology Opportunities

  1. Hyper-local weather mapping
  2. AI-based moisture prediction
  3. Offline-first mobile web apps
  4. Farmer-friendly Bangla advisories
  5. Low-bandwidth crop health scanning

Conclusion

With proper digital tools, Bangladesh can reduce food loss, increase farmer profit, and ensure long-term food security.
